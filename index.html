ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Vorleak Yek, a recent graduate eager to begin my career in web development. I hold a master’s degree in applied mathematics from CSULB (2018) and recently completed a web development bootcamp at LearningFuze in 2024.

Through the bootcamp, I honed my skills in designing with Figma and building web applications using technologies like React, JavaScript, TypeScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Tailwind. I also gained hands-on experience in backend development using Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, and deploying applications on AWS. In addition, I’m familiar with Python and have a strong foundation in programming.

My academic journey also led me to delve into fluid dynamics using MATLAB, where I developed expertise in fluid simulations. I authored a research paper titled 'Numerical investigation on the projection method for the incompressible Navier-Stokes equations on MAC grid,' further demonstrating my analytical and problem-solving abilities.
